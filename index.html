Welcome

I am a PhD student in the Department of Computer Science at Purdue University. I am interested in Computational Topology, Machine Learning and Category Theory(Sheaves, in particular). My current projects include integrating multidimensional persistence differentiably in a machine learning pipeline and combining persistence with higher order neural networks. I am fortunate to be advised by Prof. Tamal Dey.

I completed by BS-MS Dual Degree majoring in Mathematics from Indian Institute of Science Education and Research Bhopal in 2020. During my undergrad years, I have interned at Technische Universität Dresden as a DAAD WISE Scholar and at Institut de recherche mathématique avancée as a Charpak Scholar. In the industry, I have interned at Ethereum Foundation and Adobe Research.
